    Are used engines reliable and durable?

    Absolutely! Used engines can be reliable and durable, especially when sourced from reputable suppliers and well-maintained. Factors like maintenance history, mileage, and condition play a significant role. Buying from trusted sellers like Optimal Auto Parts with warranties adds peace of mind. Still, thorough research and inspections are crucial to meet your reliability and durability standards.

    If I order a used engine, what all parts will be on it?

    When you order a used engine, you'll typically get the main components needed for it to work: engine block, cylinder head(s), crankshaft, pistons, connecting rods, camshaft(s), valves, timing components, and other internal parts. Extras like intake manifold, exhaust manifold, fuel injectors, and sensors might be included based on the seller and listing. Always review the product description or contact the seller to confirm the parts included.
