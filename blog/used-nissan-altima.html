The best Car with used manual transmissions for 2024

Manual transmission cars are becoming increasingly rare, with many already gone extinct. It's undeniable that their historical significance has diminished, leaving behind only the joy of driving. Despite this, there are still around two dozen options for those who prefer manual shifting. These remaining cars are the last of their kind, and it's likely that manuals won't be around much longer. For die-hard petrol heads, we've put together a list of the top vehicles still offering manual transmission.

Chevrolet Spark

The Chevrolet Spark holds the top spot in the brand’s lineup, serving as a compact city hatchback. Alongside its primary competitor, the Nissan Versa , it stands out as one of the finest small cars with manual transmission on the market. What sets it apart is its comprehensive standard features, despite its entry-level status. The Spark impresses with smartphone compatibility and user-friendly infotainment applications, making it a standout choice in its segment.

Dodge Challenger

The Dodge Challenger defies rumors of the fading era of powerful V8 engines, remaining vibrant and strong. There's little need for elaborate praise; it simply stands as one of the premier manual muscle cars on the market. Its strengths are numerous, including ample space for up to five occupants. Notable features include its sleek design, roaring engines, and finely crafted interior. Manual transmissions are an option for the beefier trims starting from the R/T, allowing enthusiasts to personally experience the raw American power it exudes. Prepare for a lively and exhilarating ride.

Ford Bronco and Ford Bronco Sport

Locating a midsize SUV equipped with manual transmission can be a challenge due to limited options. However, apart from the Jeep Wrangler and its variants, along with select Subarus, the new Ford Bronco emerges as a compelling choice. What sets it apart is its faithful adherence to its heritage, ensuring reliability even in rugged terrain. Additionally, this SUV boasts a wide array of customization possibilities, adding to its appeal.

Hyundai Venue

We dub it the premier small SUV featuring manual transmission. Its spacious interior hosts an intuitive infotainment system that enhances user experience. Opting for higher-level trims grants access to navigation and luxurious leather upholstery, all without sacrificing safety. Lane departure warning and emergency braking systems are standard across all trims. However, its 121-horsepower engine may feel underpowered on highways, a point worth noting.

Mazda 3

The boldly designed Mazda 3 stands out as one of the top choices among manual transmission hatchbacks. Boasting lively engines, captivating design, and a meticulously crafted interior, it's hard to resist. Notably, the manual transmission is exclusive to the hatchback variant, paired with a potent 2.5L engine. What sets the Mazda 3 apart is its rarity among compact cars: the availability of all-wheel drive. It's another compelling reason to consider this standout vehicle.
